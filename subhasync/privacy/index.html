Privacy Policy

This policy covers the SubhaSync Android app and the public pages at aankda.ai/subhasync. AANKDA LLC is the publisher.

Bottom line

No accounts, analytics, ads, or crash telemetry.

SubhaSync does not create user accounts, does not run analytics, does not show ads, does not use crash telemetry, and does not sell personal data.

On-device

Daily timing and numerology run locally.

Daily timing calculations and numerology outputs are computed on your device as part of the app experience, not on an AANKDA-hosted backend.

Stored locally

Your chosen settings stay on the phone.

SubhaSync stores selected location details, timezone, saved locations and notes, theme preferences, notification preferences, and optional numerology inputs locally on your device.

Network use

Search and current location are explicit actions.

When you use city search, the entered query is sent through an AANKDA-controlled geocoding endpoint so the app can return matching locations.

When you explicitly request current location during setup, approximate coordinates may be sent through that same flow so the app can resolve a location label and timezone.

Notifications

Notifications stay local to Android.

SubhaSync uses local-only Android notifications for summaries and timing alerts. It does not rely on any remote push provider.

Retention and contact

Your data stays on-device until you remove it.

Saved data remains on your device until you clear app storage or uninstall the app.

For privacy, product, or support questions, contact AANKDA LLC at support@aankda.ai.

If these data practices change materially, this page will be updated before the changed behavior is released.
