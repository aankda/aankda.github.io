Quick answers.

Does URest collect or transmit data?

No. URest runs locally and does not send browsing data anywhere.

Why does URest need access to all sites?

To restore blocked browser behavior on the page you are visiting.

How do I disable URest on a site?

Use the popup toggle or the context menu shortcut.

Can URest break a site?

Sometimes. If a page feels off, disable URest for that site.
