Privacy policy

URest runs locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit browsing data.

This policy covers the URest extension and the official product pages under aankda.ai/urest.

Bottom line

URest does not collect, store, or transmit browsing data.

No browsing history, page content, copied text, form input, personal information, analytics, or telemetry.

Local storage

Your settings stay on your device.

Only the enabled state, excluded domains, and local counter are stored in browser extension storage.

Permissions

Site access is used to restore blocked behavior.

Content scripts run on the pages you visit to restore blocked behavior. storage keeps local settings, contextMenus provides the site shortcut, and tabs reads the active tab URL so the popup can identify the current site reliably.

Network

No extension backend or analytics pipeline.

The extension has no backend and makes no analytics or advertising requests. The product site is static and has no analytics scripts.

Scope

This policy covers URest and the official product pages.

Third-party destinations such as the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, or email links have their own policies.
